# PixelPaw - Virtual Pet App üêæ

An interactive virtual pet application built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Take care of your digital pet by feeding, playing, training, and more!

## Features

### Pet Care
- **Feed** - Keep your pet well-fed and happy
- **Play** - Increase happiness through fun activities
- **Sleep** - Restore energy and health
- **Heal** - Restore your pet's health
- **Bath** - Keep your pet clean and happy
- **Train** - Gain experience and level up

### Pet Stats
- **Health** - Overall wellbeing of your pet
- **Hunger** - How well-fed your pet is
- **Happiness** - Your pet's mood and contentment
- **Energy** - Your pet's activity level

### Progression System
- **Level System** - Your pet gains experience and levels up
- **Age Tracking** - Watch your pet grow over time
- **Mood System** - Pet's mood changes based on stats
- **Visual Feedback** - Animated reactions to actions

### Persistence
- **Auto-Save** - Game automatically saves your progress
- **Local Storage** - Progress is saved in your browser

## How to Play

1. **Name Your Pet** - Enter a name in the input field and click "Set Name"
2. **Care for Your Pet** - Use the action buttons to interact with your pet
3. **Monitor Stats** - Keep an eye on the status bars
4. **Level Up** - Gain experience through activities to level up
5. **Keep Your Pet Healthy** - Make sure all stats stay above critical levels

## Tips

- Feed your pet regularly to maintain hunger levels
- Play with your pet to keep it happy
- Put your pet to sleep when energy is low
- Train your pet to gain more experience
- Balance all activities for optimal pet health

## Technical Details

- **Pure JavaScript** - No frameworks or dependencies
- **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop and mobile
- **CSS Animations** - Smooth visual feedback
- **Local Storage** - Saves progress automatically

## Getting Started

Simply open `index.html` in your web browser. No installation or build process required!
